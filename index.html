NUDIBRANCHS

Nudibranchs (noo-dee-brawnks*) are a type of soft-bodied marine animals, noted for their bright colors and striking forms. Their nicknames include “dancer”, “dragon”, and “sea rabbit”. Casually, they are known as “sea slugs”, but not all sea slugs are nudibranchs.

*pronounciation is contested
